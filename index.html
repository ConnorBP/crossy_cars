Roady Car supports a desktop keyboard or touch controls. Use W or Up Arrow to accelerate, S or Down Arrow to brake and reverse, and A and D or the Left and Right Arrow keys to steer; Space brakes and Esc pauses. On touch devices, on-screen STEER, BRAKE, and GO zones drive the car, and tapping the screen starts, pauses, and navigates menus. Your first key press or touch also enables game audio.
